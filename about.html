Who am I?

By day, I'm a consultant in D.C. focused on data management, privacy and security. By nights and

weekends, I tap into my creativity through programming. My interests lie in web development

and UX design with a minimalist lens. With no formal instruction, I've proudly developed my skills

through self-exploration, persistant trial-and-error and an insatiable curiosity.

*

When I'm not working or coding, I can be found testing Bon Appétit recipes, dancing to Spotify

playlists or binge watching Bravo "reality" shows. I've also been known to write Spanish-language

poetry centered around my experience as a first-generation Salvadoran American. My writing can

be found in Dryland, La Horchata Zine and the feelings journal.
